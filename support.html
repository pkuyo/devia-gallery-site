Devia Gallery 是 DeviantArt 的第三方非官方客户端。如果你遇到登录、浏览、下载或收藏相关问题，可以通过下面的邮箱反馈。

联系方式

反馈邮箱：pkuyora@outlook.com

反馈时建议包含

请尽量说明你的设备型号、iOS 版本、App 版本、问题出现的页面，以及可以复现问题的操作步骤。请不要发送 DeviantArt 密码、验证码或完整 OAuth token。

常见说明

Devia Gallery 不隶属于 DeviantArt，也不能处理 DeviantArt 账号封禁、密码找回或站内付款问题。与 DeviantArt 账号本身相关的问题，请联系 DeviantArt 官方支持。

English

Devia Gallery is a third-party unofficial client for DeviantArt. If you run into sign-in, browsing, download, or collection issues, you can contact support by email.

Contact

Support email: pkuyora@outlook.com

What to Include

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, the screen where the issue happened, and steps to reproduce the issue. Do not send your DeviantArt password, verification codes, or full OAuth tokens.

Notes

Devia Gallery is not affiliated with DeviantArt and cannot resolve DeviantArt account bans, password recovery, or on-site payment issues. For account-level DeviantArt issues, contact DeviantArt official support.
