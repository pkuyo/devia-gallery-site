Devia Gallery 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 4 日 / Last updated: July 4, 2026

中文

Devia Gallery 是 DeviantArt 的第三方非官方客户端。我们不自建账户系统，不运营自己的云服务，也不会出售、出租或共享你的个人信息。

我们不收集的内容

本 App 默认不接入广告、跨应用追踪、远程分析或第三方营销 SDK。我们不会收集你的浏览历史、搜索记录、收藏夹、下载记录或设备标识并上传到我们的服务器。

保存在设备上的数据

为了保持登录和改善使用体验，App 会在本机保存 DeviantArt OAuth token、最小账户快照、图片缓存、浏览历史、搜索历史和偏好设置。这些数据用于 App 功能本身，可通过退出登录、清除缓存或清除浏览历史移除。按当前设计，token 不保存到 Keychain。

DeviantArt 登录与内容请求

登录、刷新授权、获取推荐、关注、收藏、评论、作品详情和图片内容时，App 会直接连接 DeviantArt。DeviantArt 可能根据其隐私政策处理你的账号、请求和内容访问记录。Devia Gallery 不代表 DeviantArt，也不能控制 DeviantArt 如何处理这些数据。

支持开发者与购买

如果你通过 App Store 内购支持开发者，购买流程由 Apple 处理。App 只接收购买状态，用于显示购买是否完成；支持金额不会解锁额外功能。

诊断、反馈与下载

诊断日志默认只保存在本机，并会尽量脱敏。只有你主动复制或发送反馈时，相关内容才会离开设备。保存图片时，文件会写入系统媒体库；邮件反馈会打开你的邮件 App，并由你决定是否发送。

联系我们

如果你对隐私政策或数据处理有疑问，可以发送邮件到 pkuyora@outlook.com。未来如果加入远程分析、广告、账号服务或其它数据收集能力，本政策和商店隐私披露会在发布前更新。

English

Devia Gallery is a third-party unofficial client for DeviantArt. We do not operate our own account system or cloud service, and we do not sell, rent, or share your personal information.

What We Do Not Collect

The app does not include ads, cross-app tracking, remote analytics, or third-party marketing SDKs by default. We do not collect and upload your browsing history, search history, collections, downloads, or device identifiers to our servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

To keep you signed in and improve the app experience, the app stores DeviantArt OAuth tokens, a minimal account snapshot, image cache, browsing history, search history, and preferences locally on your device. This data is used only for app features and can be removed by signing out, clearing cache, or clearing browsing history. Under the current design, tokens are not stored in Keychain.

DeviantArt Sign-In and Content Requests

When you sign in, refresh authorization, view recommendations, watching feeds, collections, comments, artwork details, or images, the app connects directly to DeviantArt. DeviantArt may process your account, requests, and content access according to its own privacy policy. Devia Gallery is not DeviantArt and cannot control how DeviantArt handles that data.

Developer Support and Purchases

If you support development through an App Store in-app purchase, Apple handles the purchase flow. The app receives purchase status only to show whether the purchase completed; support purchases do not unlock extra features.

Diagnostics, Feedback, and Downloads

Diagnostic logs stay on your device by default and are redacted where possible. They leave your device only if you copy them or send feedback yourself. Saving images writes files to the system media library; email feedback opens your mail app and is sent only if you choose to send it.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or data handling, contact pkuyora@outlook.com. If future versions add remote analytics, ads, account services, or other data collection, this policy and the store privacy disclosures will be updated before release.
